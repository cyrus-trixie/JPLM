Ways We Give

Financial giving in the church is mainly in two categories – tithe and offering. The Bible teaches that we honour God by giving 10% of our income (that’s the tithe) and with our offerings (over and above the tithe). This giving serves to take care of the day-to-day needs in the running of the church, including pastoral work, administrative support, regular purchases, general upkeep, and more.

At JPLM (Jesus Power And Life Ministries), we also have two more categories of giving that are cause-specific: our mission faith commitment goes directly to our outreach endeavours, both at home and abroad. This giving helps us to invest in our communities, support missionaries and outreach organisations, plant churches, and so much more; secondly, our project advance giving helps position the church for property and upgrade needs as they arise.

We believe that there is a blessing that God gives to us as we invest in the affairs of His Kingdom, and so we love to give at JPLM and have a deep, bible-based value for every opportunity we have to give.
